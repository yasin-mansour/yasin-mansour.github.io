What does the operation getJspContext().getOut().write(“Hi Bob”) do when called in a doTag() method? It outputs "Hi bob" string in the page. 8. What does the operation getJspBody().invoke(null) do when called in a doTag() method? When getJspBody().invoke(null) is called, it is the output resulting from the execution of the tag body's JSP content that gets passed to the client, not the JSP code itself.
